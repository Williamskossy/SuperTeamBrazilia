FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Superteam Brazil.

What is Superteam Brazil? +

Superteam Brazil is the premier community for Solana builders in Brazil. We bring together developers, designers, founders, and creators to collaborate on Web3 projects, access opportunities, and shape the future of decentralized technology in Latin America's largest market.

How do I become a member? +

Getting started is easy! Join our Telegram or Discord community, introduce yourself, and start engaging. Participate in events, contribute to discussions, complete bounties, or submit your project. Active contributors can apply for official membership to access exclusive opportunities.

What opportunities are available? +

We offer diverse opportunities including: paid bounties and freelance work, grants for new projects, full-time job placements, mentorship programs, hackathon prizes, networking with founders and investors, and access to exclusive workshops and educational content.

How can projects partner with Superteam Brazil? +

Solana projects can partner with us to access top Brazilian talent, host workshops and events, post bounties and job opportunities, sponsor hackathons, and gain visibility in the Brazilian Web3 ecosystem. Reach out via Telegram or email for partnership inquiries.

Do I need to be a developer to join? +

Not at all! While developers are core to our community, we welcome designers, content creators, marketers, community managers, business development professionals, and anyone passionate about Web3. There's a place for every skill set in the Solana ecosystem.

Ready to Build on Solana?

Join hundreds of builders, creators, and founders shaping the future of Web3 in Brazil.
